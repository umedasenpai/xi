Why Potatoes?

Whether mashed, fried, boiled, or baked, the potato is the most versatile vegetable in the known universe. It has traveled from the Andes to the stars (potatoes were the first vegetable grown in space!).

Russet

The king of fries and fluffy baked goodness.

Yukon Gold

Buttery, smooth, and perfect for mashing.

Red Bliss

Firm and waxy, ideal for potato salads.

Potato Fact of the Day

Potatoes are about 80% water and 20% solid.
